About Me

I'm a third-year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Weizhan Zhang. In 2020, I received my bachelor degree from Department of Electronic Engineering at Chongqing University. My research work has centered on networking and multimedia streaming, especially with practical applications in large-scale production systems.
