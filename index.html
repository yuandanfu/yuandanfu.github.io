About Me

I'm a third-year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Weizhan Zhang. My research work has centered on networking, content delivery, and also multimedia streaming, especially with practical applications in large-scale production systems.
