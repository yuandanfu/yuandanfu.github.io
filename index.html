About Me

I'm a second-year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Weizhan Zhang. In 2020, I received my bachelor degree from Department of Electronic Engineering at Chongqing University. I used to be a research intern at Video Cloud Team, Alibaba Cloud, during 2022-2023, supervised by Haiyu Huang. My research work has centered on networking and multimedia streaming, especially with practical applications in large-scale production systems.

Currently I am a research intern at Edge Networking Team, Alibaba Cloud, supervised by Yubing Qiu.
